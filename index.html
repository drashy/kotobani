    • Kotobani

    A simple word puzzle game writen with the Godot Engine.

    Project maintained by sammyf Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    Kotobani Copyright Sammy Fischer Licensed under GPLv3

    RULES: Form 3 letter words using adjacent tiles. the tiles must touch, but it doesn't matter where they touch. Diagonals are allowed. And you don't have to continue in the same direction to finish the word. So, if you can find an A with an R left from it, then another M ABOVE the previous R you would have a word. CLick twice on the ending letter to validate. Right mouse click restart the word. P to pause.

    The music, sound effects and the flying yoga frog are from the wonderful assets from the game Glitch and licensed under Creative Common. You can find out more at http://www.glitch.com
